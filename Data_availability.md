We used the UKBB genotype data obtained from the resource https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/
The GTEx dataset was obtained at https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/gap/cgi-bin/study.cgi?study_id=phs000424.v8.p2
The 1KG reference panel was available at https://www.cog-genomics.org/plink/2.0/resources
Summary statistics for the Height GWAS could be found at GIANT consortium at https://giant-consortium.web.broadinstitute.org/index.php/GIANT_consortium_data_files
Summary statistics for SCZ were provided by the Psychiatric Genomics Consortium (PGC) at https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/scz2022/19426775 
The Jensen tissue expression database was available at http://tissues.jensenlab.org/
